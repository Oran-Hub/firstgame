newLive: Cannon Balls 3D

Master the physics, match the colors, and blast your way to victory!

About newLive

newLive: Cannon Balls 3D is an exciting physics-based puzzle game where your goal is to shoot colorful balls from a cannon to match and eliminate targets. As you progress, the levels become more challenging, testing your aiming skills and strategic thinking.

The game features intuitive controls, allowing you to focus on your shooting strategy rather than complex mechanics. Aim your cannon, adjust your power, and launch balls to clear the levels by matching colors in this addictive 3D puzzle game.

Whether you have just a few minutes to play or want to dive into an extended gaming session, newLive offers an accessible yet challenging experience for players of all skill levels.

Game Features

Physics-Based Gameplay

Experience realistic ball physics that make each shot challenging and satisfying.

Colorful 3D Graphics

Enjoy vibrant visuals and smooth animations that bring the game to life in three dimensions.

Progressive Difficulty

Face increasingly complex challenges as you advance through numerous levels with varying layouts.

Color Matching Mechanics

Match balls of the same color to clear them and achieve high scores with strategic shots.

How to Play

Controls

  • Drag to aim your cannon and adjust shooting direction
  • Release to fire the ball
  • Tap or click the screen to shoot faster in certain levels

Objectives

  • Match and eliminate balls of the same color
  • Clear all target balls to complete each level
  • Achieve high scores by using fewer shots
  • Progress through increasingly challenging levels

Tips for Success

  • Plan your shots carefully to create chain reactions
  • Aim for clusters of the same color to clear more balls at once
  • Use the environment and physics to your advantage
  • Pay attention to ball trajectory and bounce patterns
