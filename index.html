About newLive: Goudall

Goudall is a classic platformer game for real Gameboy Color hardware. Play as wizard Goudall who must find 3 diamonds in each level to open the door to the next level. This retro-style adventure combines pixel-perfect platforming with challenging level design.

The evil wizard Walator has insidiously attacked the unsuspecting Goudall, cursing his magical power into diamonds that he hid in his underground kingdom. In order for Goudall to regain his magical power and defeat Walator, he will have to find all the diamonds.

Help Goudall explore underground caves and collect all the diamonds. Watch out for snakes, bats, hot lava, poisonous plants, giant monsters and many other traps in Waltor's underground world.

Game Features

How to Play

Controls

Keyboard Gameboy
WASD / Arrow Keys D-pad
J, Z A button
Enter Start

Objectives

  • Find all 3 diamonds in each level to open the exit door
  • Navigate through 20 increasingly difficult underground caves
  • Avoid monsters and obstacles using precise platforming
  • Recover Goudall's magical powers to defeat the evil wizard Walator

Tips for Success

  • Time your jumps carefully to avoid enemies
  • Look for hidden paths that might lead to diamonds
  • Be patient with difficult sections - practice makes perfect
  • Watch enemy patterns to find the right moment to pass them
